Chokolate Banana Sauce

Category

Main Dish

Calories

375 cal

Time

15 min

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped (optional, for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for serving)
  • 12 oz pasta of your choice

Needed

  • Frying pan
  • Toaster
  • Sharp knifes
  • Oven

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add Tomatoes and Seasoning:
    • Pour in the crushed tomatoes into the skillet.
    • Add dried oregano, dried basil, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer the Sauce:
    • Bring the sauce to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low.
    • Let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally to allow the flavors to meld.
    • Taste and adjust the seasoning. If the sauce is too acidic, you can add a teaspoon of sugar to balance it.
  5. Finish and Serve:
    • Once the sauce has thickened to your liking, remove it from heat.
    • If desired, garnish with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese before serving.
  6. Enjoy:
    • Serve the pasta hot and enjoy your delicious homemade tomato sauce pasta!
